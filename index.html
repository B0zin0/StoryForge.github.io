What's inside

Everything you need to play

StoryForge isn't just a shortcut. It's a proper launcher built from scratch with features that actually make sense for MCSM.

One-click launch

Pick your season from the home screen and hit play. The launcher finds your game, pauses the music, launches it, and resumes when you're done.

Auto game detection

Can't find where your game installed? Hit Auto-Detect and StoryForge scans your drives, Steam library, and common install paths automatically.

Mod manager

Install, enable, disable, and remove mods without touching game files manually. Supports textures, scripts, audio, archives and more.

Background music

The launcher plays the MCSM theme on the home screen. Music pauses when you launch the game and comes back automatically when you close it.

Auto update checker

StoryForge checks GitHub for new releases on startup and shows a banner if there's an update waiting. You never have to check manually.

Discord Rich Presence

Shows what season you're playing in your Discord status. Updates automatically when you launch and clears when you're done.
