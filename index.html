What's inside

Everything you need to play

StoryForge isn't just a shortcut. It's a proper launcher built from scratch with features that actually make sense for MCSM.

🎮

One-click launch

Pick your season from the home screen and hit play. The launcher finds your game, launches it, and resumes when you're done.

🔍

Auto game detection

Can't find where your game installed? Hit Auto-Detect and StoryForge scans your drives, Steam library, and common install paths to find it automatically.

🧩

Mod manager

Install, enable, disable, and remove mods without touching any game files manually. Supports textures, scripts, audio, archives and more.

🎵

Background music

The launcher plays the MCSM theme while you're on the home screen. Volume slider included. Music pauses when you launch the game and comes back when you close it.

🔔

Auto update checker

StoryForge checks GitHub for new releases on startup and shows a banner if there's an update waiting. You never have to check manually.

🎮

Discord Rich Presence

Shows what season you're playing in your Discord status. Automatically updates when you launch a game and clears when you close it.
